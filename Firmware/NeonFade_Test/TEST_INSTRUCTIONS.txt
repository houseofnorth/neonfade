═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  NeonFade PCB — Hardware Test Instructions
  Firmware: NeonFade_Test
  Board: Neon Fader BRD mkIII v367
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

EQUIPMENT
  - Multimeter (DC voltage)
  - USB-C cable (to XIAO ESP32C3)
  - Serial monitor @ 115200 baud
  - Mains supply (230V) OR bench 5V on 5V_SYS / GND if mains not available

BLACK PROBE: TP '5-' (GND) throughout all measurements.


───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 1 — Power rails  (PSU only, no MCU needed)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Apply mains power. Do NOT plug in USB yet.

  TP      Net         Expected        Pass if
  ─────   ─────────   ───────────     ───────────────────────
  5+      +5V_SYS     5.0 V           4.75 – 5.25 V
  5-      GND         0 V             reference

Fail: PSU not outputting — check fuse, check RECOM orientation.


───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 2 — MCU boot  (plug in USB-C, open serial monitor)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Serial should print:

  === NeonFade Test Firmware ===
  Sweep pot full range to calibrate (15 s timeout).

LED_A (D7) fast-blinks — waiting for calibration.
LED_B (D8) OFF.

  TP      Net         Expected        Pass if
  ─────   ─────────   ───────────     ───────────────────────
  5+      +5V_SYS     5.0 V           unchanged from Step 1
  9       D9/MISO     ~3.3 V          3.1 – 3.4 V (idle GPIO)
  10      D10/MOSI    ~3.3 V          3.1 – 3.4 V (idle GPIO)
  1       D1          ~3.3 V          3.1 – 3.4 V (idle GPIO)

Fail: No serial output — check USB driver / board selection (XIAO ESP32C3).


───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 3 — Relay toggle  (starts immediately on boot)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
No calibration needed. Relay toggles every 1 second from boot.
You will hear/feel the relay clicking.

  TP      Net         Relay OFF       Relay ON
  ─────   ─────────   ───────────     ───────────────────────
  R       R (coil-)   ~5.0 V          ~0.1 V

Serial column RLY alternates OFF / ON every second.
LED_A mirrors relay: ON when relay energised.

Fail: TP R stuck at 5V — check IC2 (MMBT3904), R10, D6 connection.
Fail: TP R stuck at 0V — check relay coil continuity / K1 solder joints.


───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 4 — Pot sweep calibration
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Within 15 seconds of boot, slowly turn the motorpot
from full left to full right (full mechanical travel).

Watch serial — MIN and MAX columns should track your sweep.

Serial will print when done:
  Calibrated: min=NNN  max=NNN  range=NNN

  TP      Net         Expected during sweep
  ─────   ─────────   ───────────────────────────────────────
  W1      WIP1        0 – 3.3 V, sweeps smoothly with pot
  W2      DIM2        0 – 3.3 V, mirrors W1

Range should be > 2000 ADC counts for a valid calibration.
If timeout fires (15 s), firmware uses defaults (150 – 3900).

Fail: W1 stuck — check R5 (1kΩ) and pot Gang A wiring.
Fail: W2 stuck — check pot Gang B wiring to J1.


───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 5 — Motor oscillation  (starts after calibration)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Motor begins driving between calibrated MIN and MAX automatically.

  TP      Net         Motor idle      Motor running
  ─────   ─────────   ───────────     ───────────────────────
  M1      MTR2        0 V             PWM ~0–5 V (one direction)
  M2      MTR1        0 V             PWM ~0–5 V (other direction)

Only ONE of M1/M2 is active at a time. They alternate as the
motor reverses. Measure with DC setting — meter will read
average voltage proportional to duty (~3.9 V at speed 200/255).

Serial MOT column shows: FORWARD / REVERSE / STOP
LED_B: ON when FORWARD, OFF when REVERSE.

Pot wiper (W1) should sweep back and forth in sync with motor travel.

Fail: Motor not moving — check L293DD (IC1) solder joints, VM rail.
Fail: Motor runs one way only — check both PWM channels on M1 and M2.
Fail: Motor runs but doesn't reverse — pot feedback broken (re-check W1).


───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 6 — I2C bus  (OLED connector J2)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Measure at J2 Qwiic connector or R6/R7 top pads.

  Pin     Net         Expected (idle, no OLED)
  ─────   ─────────   ───────────────────────
  SDA     D4          3.3 V (pulled up via R6 4.7kΩ)
  SCL     D5          3.3 V (pulled up via R7 4.7kΩ)
  VCC     +3V3        3.3 V
  GND     GND         0 V

Fail: SDA/SCL low — check R6/R7 solder joints.


───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SERIAL MONITOR — column reference
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  POT    — raw ADC reading (0–4095)
  MIN    — lowest ADC seen since boot
  MAX    — highest ADC seen since boot
  MOTOR  — FORWARD / REVERSE / STOP
  RELAY  — ON / OFF (1 Hz toggle)
  CALIB  — ... = waiting,  OK = calibrated


───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PASS CRITERIA SUMMARY
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  [ ] 5V rail: 4.75 – 5.25 V
  [ ] 3.3V idle GPIOs (1, 9, 10): 3.1 – 3.4 V
  [ ] Relay clicking at 1 Hz; TP R swings 5V ↔ 0.1V
  [ ] Pot sweep: W1 and W2 follow pot travel 0–3.3V
  [ ] Calibration message printed, range > 2000
  [ ] Motor oscillates; M1/M2 alternate PWM
  [ ] W1 sweeps in sync with motor travel
  [ ] SDA/SCL idle at 3.3V
